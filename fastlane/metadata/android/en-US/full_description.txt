Simple open-source app to stream music from your own Subsonic or Navidrome servers or your phone's storage.

Chora has a very simple and modern UI, following Material 3 guidelines, and a native Android TV UI, with support for Android Auto aswell.

Important: Chora does not provide any music by itself. It's merely a front-end for Subsonic servers and/or local storage, meaning the end user has to provide the music files.
